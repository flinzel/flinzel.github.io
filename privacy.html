3. What data Domiq processes

3.1 Data you enter in the app

Domiq stores and displays content you create locally on your device.

Based on the current project setup, the content you create in Domiq is stored only locally on your device. The app currently does not use cloud sync and does not store your app content on the provider’s own servers. No personal data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3.3 Files and documents

If you link files or documents to entries in the app, Domiq processes the related file information on your device as needed to provide that feature.

3.4 Website technical data

When you visit this website, the hosting provider may process technically necessary server log data, such as IP address, date and time of access, requested page, browser information, and error messages. This is done to provide and secure the website. This website is hosted via GitHub Pages. The hosting provider may process technical data as described above.

3.5 In-app purchases

If you start a subscription or restore a purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details, only the information needed to determine purchase status and app access.

3.6 No analytics, no tracking

Based on the current project setup, Domiq does not use analytics services, advertising tracking, or cloud backends operated by the provider.
